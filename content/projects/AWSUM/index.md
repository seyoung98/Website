### Project Overview
**Ewha Womans University Computer Science Capstone Project:** 
  AI-Online Assistant with Lecture Summarization

* **Role:** Lead Developer for Natural Language Processing (NLP)
* **Duration:** Sep 2021 – Jun 2022
* **Key Achievement:** Developed a full-stack AI service to enhance digital learning efficiency during the COVID-19 pandemic.

---

### Problem Definition
The shift to online education led to a decrease in student concentration and difficulty in grasping core concepts from vast amounts of video content. There was a critical need for a tool that could effectively bridge the gap between digital content and learner comprehension.

### Service Description: "AWESUM"
AWESUM is an AI-powered educational assistant that provides **Automatic Summarization**, **Keyword Extraction**, and **Quiz Generation**. By converting long lecture videos into digestible summaries, it allows students to learn more efficiently in a digital-first environment.


---

### My Key Contributions
* **Lecture Summarization Model Implementation:**
    * Fine-tuned **BERT-based extractive summarization** models to identify core sentences from transcripts.
    * Applied **BERT-T5 generative models** to produce natural, learner-friendly summaries, improving upon basic extraction methods.
* **Automated Knowledge Assessment:**
    * Developed a **T5-based Question Generation Algorithm** to automatically create True/False (OX) quizzes.
    * Implemented a "False Sentence Generation" technique to challenge and verify student understanding.
* **System Integration:**
    * Collaborated on integrating NLP models with a Django-based web backend and deploying via AWS to ensure service scalability.

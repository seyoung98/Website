* **Achievement:** Developed a personalized fashion discovery system to solve limitations in traditional collaborative filtering.

---

### Problem Definition
As the online fashion market grew rapidly, users faced difficulties in navigating vast inventories. Traditional collaborative filtering suffered from the **'Cold Start' problem**, where new items or users without history could not be effectively recommended. There was a critical need for a system that could suggest items based purely on their visual content.

### Proposed Solution
We developed a **Content-Based Recommendation System** that analyzes the visual features of clothing items. By extracting technical "descriptors" directly from images, the system can recommend similar styles immediately, even for brand-new items.


---

### My Key Contributions
* **Visual Feature Extraction:** * Utilized **CNN (Convolutional Neural Networks)** to convert four key clothing attributes into high-dimensional feature vectors.
    * Engineered a custom **Color Feature Module** that calculates average RGB values from cropped images, overcoming the limitations of standard models in capturing precise color information.
* **Advanced Recommendation Algorithm:**
    * Implemented **Cosine Similarity** to match user inputs with the MUSINSA dataset, enhancing recommendation accuracy for personalized fashion discovery.
